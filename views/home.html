Ides of March

John Wilkes as Mark Antony gives a rousing yet solemn speech following an undoubtedly accurate reenactment of Caesar's death. All attendees are silent out of reverence and respect for Ms. Conklin's dear spouse.

Induction Ceremony

In appropriate clothing, Ms. Conklin holds the spirits of Latin gingerly as Antonius Carrington proceeds to light one of them on fire. This ceremony is so exclusive and secret that we had to share it with you online.

Myth Pumpkins

As Halloween draws close, TJ students are sharing in the revelry with a Roman twist: decorating pumpkins with mythological stories and creatures! Come look at the best in show, funniest, and most artistic pumpkins later.

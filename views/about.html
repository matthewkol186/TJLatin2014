About TJ Latin Honor Society

Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (TJ) is a Virginia Governor's School and a founding member of the National Consortium for Specialized Secondary Schools of Mathematics, Science and Technology. TJ has been awarded the title of #1 high school in the US by US News multiple times, and continues to top the list in the STEM Category. This year, TJ was also awarded the title of #1 high school by Newsweek.
The Latin Club, together with the Latin Honor Society and Certamen teams, is one of the most active academic clubs at TJ. We host fun Rome-related activities as well as rigorous tests - check out our pictures and awards! We are an extremely spirited community, consistently taking the top spots for spirit at state and national conventions. There's a multitude of opportunities for anyone interested in Latin to engage with LHS. If you like academics, you'll enjoy taking part in the myriad tests we take during the year, many of which our students win national awards for. If you enjoy artwork, you can submit it for prizes at convention and take part in various art-related Latin Honor Society activities hosted throughout the year. Even if you just like to have fun, the Roman holidays we celebrate are both educational and a blast to attend!
